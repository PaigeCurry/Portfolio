Hi! My name is Paige and I am a college student with a passion for art, character design, and visual storytelling. I enjoy experimenting with different art styles and learning new skills.

Many of my artistic interests revolve around character design, worldbuilding, and the visual storytelling found in modern games and animation. I am especially inspired by artists who combine strong design with immersive storytelling.

♡︎ Digital Art and Character Design ♡︎

Digital art allows artists to combine their creativity with technology, making it possible to design characters and entire worlds. Not to mention, being able to undo mistakes is a life saver!

Why Character Design Interests Me

Character design combines visual creativity and storytelling. I love the idea of something I made being brought to life and given a personality and a story to tell.

How Games and Animation Influence My Work

Video games and animation inspire many of my ideas. Seeing how artists build unique characters motivates me to continue developing my own artistic style. I hope to learn 3D modeling soon, and expand my skill set.
